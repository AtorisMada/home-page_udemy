My best movies by Adam

My top 3 movies all the time

Pulp Fiction

The film intertwines four stories of crime and violence in Los Angeles, featuring hitmen, a gangster's wife, a boxer, and two diner bandits. The narrative is non-linear and filled with eclectic dialogue and dark humor

Goodfellas

Based on the true story of Henry Hill, the film chronicles his life in the mob from the 1950s to the 1980s, focusing on his relationships with his wife and mob partners. The film is known for its detailed portrayal of the Mafia lifestyle and its sudden bursts of violence.

Reservoir Dogs

The film follows a group of diamond thieves who carry out a jewelry store heist that goes terribly wrong. The story is told in a nonlinear fashion, with flashbacks revealing the events leading up to and following the heist
